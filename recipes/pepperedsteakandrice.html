Peppered Steak and Rice

Description

This dish is a firm favourite in our house, loved by all. Made with diced cuts of beef, slow cooked West Indian style using traditional seasoning accompanied with basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked basmati white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced and chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced and chopped
  • 750g boneless sirloin steak, diced
  • 2 teaspoon Dark Soy
  • 1 tablespoon oxtail seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose seasoning
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons gravy granules
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Knob of butter

Steps

  1. Place rice and 2 cups water in a medium saucepan, and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes. add Knob of butter, stir in when melted.
  2. Prepare steak by way of washing with lime or vinegar then add oxtail and all purpose seasonings to the meat as well as the salt, black pepper and dark soy . Massage the mixture into the meat well and leave to marinate for 30 minutes in the fridge.
  3. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat, and saute onion and green bell pepper until tender.
  4. Add steak and stir into the onion and green bell pepper mixture. Cook and stir until steak is evenly browned. Mix in 1 1/2 cups water, more if needed, cover and slow cook until meat is tender. Once cooked, bring to the boil and add gravy to suit.
  5. Simply serve over a bed of basmati rice, crunchy roasties a welcome additon!
